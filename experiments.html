Experiments

These are my personal experiments where I document and track various small projects and hobbies. Each experiment focuses on observing, measuring, and discovering.

  • Hydroponic Basil Growth

    Comparing growth rates of basil using deep water culture vs. Kratky method. Tracking nutrient levels and light exposure over a six-week period to determine which method produces the most robust plants with optimal flavor profiles.

  • Sourdough Starter Day/Night Feeding

    Observing the activity differences in a sourdough starter fed consistently in the morning versus one fed in the evening. Measured rise times and pH levels over several cycles to determine if feeding time affects fermentation characteristics and final bread texture.

  • Local Bird Feeder Visitors

    Cataloging bird species visiting a specific feeder over a month (Spring 2024). Noted times of day, preferred seeds (sunflower vs. mixed), and interactions between species. Created a small database to track patterns and seasonal variations.

  • Kombucha Second Fermentation Flavors

    Testing various fruit combinations (ginger-lemon, raspberry-mint, blueberry-basil) for second fermentation of kombucha. Rated flavor intensity, carbonation level, and overall appeal after 3 days of fermentation at room temperature.
